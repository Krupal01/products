# 💧 SmartSip – Smart Hydration Tracking App  

SmartSip is a modern hydration tracking app designed to help you stay healthy and energized by maintaining your daily water intake goals.

With smart reminders, hydration goal tracking, analytics, and IoT device integration (ESP32 support), SmartSip makes it effortless to build better hydration habits.

## 🚀 Features

### 🔌 ESP32 Device Integration
- Connect to your SmartSip hardware (ESP32-based device)
- Monitor device battery level
- Track hydration data from your smart bottle

### ⏱ Smart Water Reminders
- Set custom reminder intervals
- Start/Stop reminders anytime
- Test notifications instantly
- Get notified throughout the day to stay hydrated

### 🎯 Hydration Goals
- Set daily water intake goals (in liters)
- Automatic monthly goal calculation
- Real-time goal balance tracking
- Simple and intuitive goal management

### 📊 Analytics Dashboard
- Track your hydration history
- Visual progress insights
- Monitor daily and monthly performance
